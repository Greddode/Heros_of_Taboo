Heroes of Taboo — ALPHA-0.0.7 Devlog

Ranged & Magic Combat, Smarter AI, and Polish

What's New in 0.0.7

This update brings two big features: ranged and magic attack types (with projectiles!) and a major AI overhaul that makes monsters smarter and more dangerous. Plus a handful of quality-of-life fixes.

Ranged & Magic Combat

Combat is no longer just bump-to-attack. Two monster types now attack from a distance:

Goblin Archer

  • New monster type: Goblin Archer (HP 5, ATK 2, DEF 0, EXP 14)
  • Fires a pixel-line projectile in cardinal directions
  • Range 5 tiles — if you're in its sights, it will shoot before you can close the gap
  • Appears from floor 2 onward
  • Sprite: Goblin_Archer.png

Warp Skull (Magic)

  • The Warp Skull now uses magic attacks instead of melee
  • Range 3 tiles, cardinal direction only
  • Fires an animated tileset projectile — wave-animated frames ripple from caster to target
  • Uses 9 animation frames from magic_attacks.png (GID 31–39, row 3)
  • Each tile along the Bresenham line shows a different frame based on wave propagation

Technical Details

  • New AttackType enum: ATTACK_MELEE, ATTACK_RANGED, ATTACK_MAGIC
  • attackType and attackRange fields added to both MonsterTemplate and Monster structs
  • Projectile system — Projectile struct tracks start/end tiles, attack type, animation frames, and color
  • Projectile timers — projectileTimer/projectileDuration count down each frame, projectile auto-clears on expiry (0.25s)
  • Enemy turn hold — the animatingEnemyTurn flag delays the player turn transition until all projectile and monster movement animations finish
  • Ranged sound effects — loaded from resources/audio/sounds/ranged_attack/
  • Magic sound effects — loaded from resources/audio/sounds/magic_attack/
  • Magic texture — magicAttacksTexture loaded from resources/tilesets/magic_attacks.png (640×576, 10 cols, 64×64 tiles)

Monster AI Rebalance

Monsters are now smarter and more persistent. The old simple chase-or-wander behaviour has been replaced with a three-tier AI system:

Hunt Memory

  • Monsters remember your last known position for 4 turns after losing line of sight
  • They'll chase you around corners and through corridors
  • The lastSeenX/lastSeenY fields track the player's position; huntTurns counts down each turn without LOS, resetting to 4 when the player is re-spotted

Multi-Attempt Wander

  • When wandering, monsters now try up to 4 random directions before giving up (previously just 1 attempt)
  • This means monsters navigate around obstacles instead of standing still when their first pick is blocked
  • Wander range expanded to detectionRange + 8 (was +4)

Refactored Movement

  • Movement logic extracted into two helper functions: MoveToward() and ApplyMove()
  • MoveToward() evaluates all 4 cardinal directions and picks the one closest to the target
  • ApplyMove() handles the actual tile update and facing direction
  • This cleaned up the AI function significantly and reduced code duplication between chase and hunt phases

Bug Fixes & Polish

  • Sprint potion pickup — sprinting over a potion tile now correctly picks it up (was only working with normal step-by-step movement)
  • Monster_Spawn restoration — fixed a regression where m->alive, m->active, m->facingRight, m->name, and m->expValue were accidentally dropped when inserting new fields
  • Missing Goblin template fields — added missing .attackType/.attackRange to the Goblin template (defaulting to melee)
